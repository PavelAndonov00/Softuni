ITCSS -> 7 levels inverted triangle
1. Settings (Variables, configuration of the application)
2. Tools (Mixin and functions)
3. Generic (Normalize and reset)
4. Elements (Default styles of tags)
5. Object (Some container, some separate part)
6. Components (The most reusable parts)
7. Utilities (The most specific things, some overrides and etcetera)
